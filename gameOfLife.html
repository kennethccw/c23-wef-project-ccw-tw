Rules:
  1. If a box has life and it has less than neighbors. It dies of loneliness. The box becomes lifeless next generation.
  2. If a box has life and it has more than neighbors. It dies of overpopulation. The box becomes lifeless next generation.
  3. If a box has no life and it has neighbors, the box in next generation fills with life because of reproduction.
